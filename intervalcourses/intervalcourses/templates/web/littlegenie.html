Why Choose
Little Genie?

At Little Genie, your kid will have an early education that sparks their curiosity, creativity, and confidence. Our program blends playful learning with expert guidance, creating a foundation that helps children thrive academically, socially, and emotionally.

Book a demo class today

Play-Based Learning

Play-based learning combines purposeful play with guided instruction, nurturing curiosity and foundational skills in young children through joyful, hands-on activities that make learning feel like play.

Safe and Nurturing Environment

We provide a secure, warm, and inspiring virtual space for children to express themselves, explore, and develop.

Expert Educators

We have a team of passionate and professionally trained teachers who adapt themselves to each child's unique learning style, ensuring personalized attention and support.

High-quality study materials

We provide our little munchkins with a range of research-backed, fun study materials to boost their language, comprehension, hygiene, and scientific thinking abilities while keeping them engaged.

Parent Collaboration

Regular updates and progress reports keep parents informed and involved in the child's educational journey.

Certification Upon Completion

Children who complete the one-year Little Genie program will receive a Completion Certificate that recognizes their progress and readiness for their next academic stage.

Core Learning Areas

Language & Communication

Reading, writing, rhymes, and storytelling to build vocabulary and literacy.

Math & Problem-Solving

Interactive games introducing numbers, counting, shapes, and measurements.

Social & Emotional Development

Encouraging teamwork, sharing, and communication to ensure the social & emotional development of kids.

Science & Discovery

Encouraging observation, questioning, and understanding of the world around us to develop scientific thinking in children

Creativity & Imagination

Exposure to arts, music, dance, and role-playing activities to foster creativity and imagination

Physical & Motor Skills
