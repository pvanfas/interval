How to Calculate Your Percentage in Tamilnadu Class 12

Formula: Percentage = (Total Marks Obtained / Maximum Marks) × 100

Example:

  • Subjects: 6
  • Marks: 195, 187, 191, 188, 193, 190
  • Total Marks = 1144
  • Maximum Marks = 6 × 200 = 1200
  • Percentage = (1144 / 1200) × 100 = 95.33%
