How to Calculate Your Tamil Nadu SSLC Percentage

To calculate your percentage in the Tamil Nadu SSLC exam, follow these simple steps:

  1. Identify the Marks Scored: Enter the marks you received in each of the five subjects:
    • Language
    • English
    • Mathematics
    • Science
    • Social Science
  2. Add the Marks: Sum up the marks you got in all five subjects.
  3. Use the Formula:
    Percentage = (Total Marks Scored ÷ 500) × 100
  4. Example: If your total marks are 445 out of 500:
    Percentage = (445 ÷ 500) × 100 = 89.00%

This method gives you your overall SSLC percentage based on your marks out of 500.
