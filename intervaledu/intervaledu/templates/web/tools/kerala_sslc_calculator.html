Kerala SSLC Grade to Percentage Calculator 2025

If you're looking to estimate your Kerala SSLC exam percentage based on the grades you received, we have an easy way to help. The Kerala SSLC grading system is grade-based, not mark-based, and each grade has a midpoint percentage that you can use for calculation.

Try Our Free Kerala SSLC Grade to Percentage Calculator

Simply input the number of subjects you received each grade in, and our free calculator will give you an estimated percentage. It’s a quick and easy way to understand your performance!

Disclaimer

This calculator gives an estimated percentage based on the midpoints of the grades. It may not match the official results, as the Kerala SSLC mark sheet only shows grades and not exact percentages or marks.

How to Calculate Your Estimated Percentage

You’ll need to enter the number of subjects you scored each grade in. The following midpoint values are used for the calculation:

Grade Range (%) Midpoint Used for Calculation
A+ 90% and above 95%
A 80% – 89% 85%
B+ 70% – 79% 75%
B 60% – 69% 65%
C+ 50% – 59% 55%
C 40% – 49% 45%
D+ 30% – 39% 35%
D 20% – 29% 25%
E Below 20% 15%

To calculate your SSLC percentage, add the number of subjects you received each grade in, multiply each by the respective midpoint value, and divide the total by 10

The midpoint is an average value used to represent each grade’s percentage range. For example, for A+ (90% and above), the midpoint used is 95.

If you score below a D+ grade (3 points) in any subject, you must appear for the SAY (Save A Year) exam to get another chance to pass.
